Hello! I'm Kassidy Shedd, and I recently graduated with a Master of Science in Robotics from Northwestern University in September 2024. Prior to that, I earned my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from New Mexico State University in 2023.

I'm particularly passionate about the intersection of robotics, healthcare, and rehabilitation, with a focus on surgical robots, exo-skeletons, companion robots, autonomous systems, and the integration of robotics with computer vision for real-world applications.
Throughout my academic career, I have gained experience in key areas such as ROS/ROS2, MoveIt, Python, C++, and machine learning models.

My goal is to continue developing innovative robotic systems that can enhance quality of life, particularly in healthcare and rehabilitation.
